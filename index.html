FAQ

Who are your classes for?

Our classes are for kids (ages 10-17) who want to start coding from scratch or want to further develop their skills.

What programming languages do you cover?

I teach foundational programming concepts using Scratch for kids and move on to Python as the topics advance.

Do I need any prior programming experience?

No, our courses are designed for beginners with no prior experience.

How are the classes structured?

Each class will include 3 hours of live sessions per week, offered as either two 90-minute sessions or one 3-hour session with a 15-minute break. Optional assignments will be provided after each session, and students will receive full support outside of class.

How do I sign up for a class?

You can contact us through the website's contact form, from there we'll will provide more details about the current class schedules and sign up!

Are the classes online or in person?

Currently, all classes are online, using video conferencing and interactive coding tools.

How much does it cost?

Our current rate is just $10 per hour (USD), totaling $30 per week.

Do you offer free trial classes?

Yes! We host a 90-minutes free trial class for beginners to experience the learning style periodically.

What tools or software do I need?

For beginner courses, all you need to start is a computer with internet access. Any required software for future sessions will be communicated in advance and provided from official, trusted sources.

Will I get hands-on coding experience?

Yes! All lessons are interactive, with real coding exercises and projects, we also provide students with optional weekly coding assignments.

Can parents monitor their child's progress?

Yes! Parents can choose to receive monthly updates and can join major project presentations.

What if I miss a class?

If you miss a class we'll provide study material and support for the missed subjects so you can catch up, plus the class won't be charged.
