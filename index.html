FAQ

Who are your classes for?

Our classes are for kids (ages 12-17) who want to start coding from scratch or want to further develop their skills.

What programming languages do you cover?

I teach foundational programming concepts using Scratch for kids and move on to Python as the topics advance.

How are the classes structured?

Our offer is a 90 minutes session per week plus asynchronous exercises and projects, contact us to check the current class openings available.

How do I sign up for a class?

You can contact us through the website's contact form, from there we'll will provide more details about the current class openings and sign up!

Are the classes online or in person?

Currently, all classes are online, using video conferencing and interactive coding tools.

How much does it cost?

Our current rate is just $50 per month.

Do you offer free trial classes?

Yes! We host a 90-minutes free trial class for beginners to experience the learning style periodically.

What tools or software do I need?

For beginner courses, all you need to start is a computer with internet access. Any required software for future sessions will be communicated in advance and provided links will be given from official, trusted sources.

Will my child get hands-on coding experience?

Yes! All lessons are interactive, with real coding exercises and projects, we also provide students with optional weekly coding assignments.

Can parents monitor their child's progress?

Yes! Parents can choose to receive monthly updates and can join their children at our project presentations as viewers.
